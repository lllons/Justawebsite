About Lorenzo

TRAIN SMART.
MOVE FREE.

I'm Lorenzo — a certified personal trainer running outdoor fitness classes across Auckland. My sessions are built around your body and the environment around you. No crowded gyms, no expensive equipment. Just results.

Every session is designed around calisthenics — the art of using your own bodyweight as resistance. It's one of the oldest and most effective forms of training on the planet.

What is Calisthenics?

Calisthenics uses your own bodyweight — push-ups, pull-ups, dips, squats, planks — to build genuine functional strength, flexibility, and endurance. You develop real control over your body, not just size for the mirror.
